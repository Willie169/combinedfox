## combinedfox

`user.js` combining [arkenfox](https://github.com/arkenfox/user.js), [Betterfox](https://github.com/yokoffing/Betterfox), and combinedfox overrides for Firefox and its forks on deskop, and instructions for Firefox and its forks on desktop and Android, for privacy, security, and convenience.

Do not trust any site that claim to be combinedfox. This repo is the only official source.

The combinedfox user.js combines Arkenfox, Betterfox, and combinedfox overrides and is designed for Firefox and its forks on deskop. Read the [wiki](https://github.com/Willie169/combinedfox/wiki) for how to use combinedfox `user.js` and instructions about Firefox and its forks on desktop and Android.
